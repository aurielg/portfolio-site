About Me.

Hi! My name is Auriel and I am freelance web developer and designer living in Wilmington, Delaware. My path is a little different from your average techie... I have a liberal arts degree, studied sociology (not the most practical major you might be thinking), and was headed to law school. But after testing the waters in tech, I haven't looked back! I love combining my analytical and creative abilities to build eye-catching and efficient websites. My design aesthetic is very clean, precise and contemporary, but I also love experimenting with bold and innovative concepts. In addition to coding and designing, I also enjoy blogging, photography, traveling, and yoga.
